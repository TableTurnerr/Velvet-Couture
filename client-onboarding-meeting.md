
# Client Onboarding Meeting — Al-Baghdady Restaurant & Bakery

  
## Meeting goals
  
1. Questions about the brand, understand the audience, objectives and priorities.

2. Get ownership/access for the assets we need: domain, Google Business Profile, hosting, analytics.

3. Real menu data, Media, other content.

4. What to expect

---

  

## Questions to ask

  

### 1. Brand consolidation (the big one)

  

- The grader found four name variants. Which is the **legal trading name** and which is the **public-facing name** you want shown everywhere? (Currently the site uses "Al-Baghdady Restaurant & Bakery".)

- Correct Domain? we found 3, do you still have access or should we try to take those down? - Important for SEO

- There are three phone numbers floating around, correct phone number?

- Do you have a domain email address? What is the correct address customers should use for general enquiries? For catering?
  

### 2. Access and ownership

  

- Who owns the domain registrar account for `al-baghdady.com`? We need access to that (or want you to enter our DNS codes)

- Have you shared **Google Business Profile** with the team?

- Social accounts: Instagram, Facebook are these all you want to add on the site?

- Is there an existing Google Analytics or Search Console property we should connect to, or do we set up fresh ones?
  

### 3. Menu
  

- Where can we see your latest menu? preferably on some website like your delievry partner.

- Any Popular Dishes?
  

### 4. Delivery and operations

- Do you take **reservations**?

-  **Delivery**: Only through PostMates or any others aswell? Any plans on in-house delievries?

-  **Catering orders**: Do you even do catering orders, any plans to, how are you doing it right now? 

  

### 5. Local SEO and neighborhoods

  

- The site has `/near/[city]` pages for 7 neighborhoods (see `neighborhoods.ts`). Are those the actual areas you serve / want to attract customers from?

- Are there neighborhoods missing? Any that shouldn't be there?

- Who are the 2–3 competitors customers compare you to? (Useful for keyword targeting, not for the site copy.)

  

### 6. Photography and brand assets

  

- Do you have a **logo** in vector format (SVG, AI, EPS)? PNG is a fallback but we want vector.

- Are there professional photos of food, interior, staff? Ones you sent seemed really nice do you have pictures of all

- Upload to our Drive if you have more images than the ones you emailed.

  

### 7. Reviews and testimonials

  

- We keep 3 star and below saved locally, 4+ go to google.

  

### 8. Audience


- Tell me **a bit about the customers**
  

### 9. Ongoing maintenance

  

- Who will tell us when the menu changes, prices update, hours shift?

- Decision-making: is the owner the only sign-off, or are there partners / a manager?

- Preferred communication channel and response time expectations (WhatsApp, email, weekly call)?

- We will send you monthly reports and discuss them with you aswell, SEO significant traction takes around 2-3 months. We will start showing up on top of our own restaurant's search very soon, within the first month
  

### 10. Next Meeting

- After we finish the site, next 4-8 days, we want to go live as soon as possible.
